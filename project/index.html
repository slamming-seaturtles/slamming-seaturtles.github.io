About Me

I'm Sam. I am a sophomore Marine Science major. I've got two dogs (one of them doesn't live with me currently) and two cats. I am trying to find my own place to live as I am currently living with my parents 30 minutes away from Columbia. I was an average A/B student throughout grade school and managed to graduate high school with a 4.6 GPA. Some things about me: I have a bit of a spending problem except when I know that I'm tight on money, I hyperfixate on things often and then drop them suddenly, and I say I read a lot but I really don't... I do enjoy reading though.

I'm from a military family, so I've moved around more than the average person. I've lived in Mississippi, Missouri and South Carolina. I was born in Mississippi in the middle of November 2002, but have been living in South Carolina for about 10 years. Growing up, I went to 5 different elementary schools, 3 middle schools, and 1 high school. This used to be my personality, not going to lie, especially going through middle school in a small town where no one travels outside the state much.

My Future Career Plans

Now, I'm just focusing on figuring out graduate school plans and contemplating my plans for my careers. Rehabilitation has been my dream since I was a kid, but being able to learn more through research is also very appealing.
